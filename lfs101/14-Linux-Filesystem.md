# Linux Filesystem:

Different types of filesystems supported by Linux:

    * **Conventional disk filesystems**:
        1. ext3
        1. ext4
        1. XFS
        1. Btrfs
        1. JFS
        1. NTFS
        1. vfat
        1. exfat

    * **Flash storage filesystems**:
        1. ubifs
        1. jffs2
        1. yaffs

    * **Special purpose filesystems**:
        1. procfs
        1. sysfs
        1. tmpfs
        1. squashfs
        1. debugfs
        1. fuse
